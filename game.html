Pixel Miner: Deep Dig

Score: 0
Energy: 100%
Time: 60s

Objective:

Help the mining robot dig through the underground terrain to collect gems for points and diamonds to extend time while avoiding dangerous lava pocket traps. Maximize your score before running out of time or energy! The deeper you dig, the harder it gets!

Controls:

  • Up Arrow: Move up
  • Down Arrow: Move down
  • Left Arrow: Move left
  • Right Arrow: Move right

Rules:

  • Scoring:
    • Collect Gems to earn 10 points.
    • Collect Diamonds to earn 50 points and gain an extra 10 seconds of time.
  • Energy: Every move costs 5 energy points. Running out of energy ends the game.
  • Traps: Stepping on a Lava Pocket trap ends the game immediately.
  • Timer: You start with 60 seconds. Time runs out unless you collect diamonds to extend it.
  • Collapsing Terrain: Random tiles may collapse as you dig, so plan your moves carefully.
